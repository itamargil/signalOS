# SignalOS — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

SignalOS ("we", "the tool") is an internal market-research tool that aggregates
public signals from social platforms to evaluate product ideas. This policy
describes what we access, how we use it, and how we retain it. Replace the
bracketed contact details before publishing.

## What we access
- **Public content only** from Reddit, X, and Instagram: posts/threads, their
  public text, public engagement counts (upvotes, likes, comments, views), public
  author handles, and timestamps.
- We do **not** access private messages, non-public profiles, follower lists,
  email addresses, or any non-public personal information.

## How we use it
- To compute **aggregate** market signal — recurring themes, pain points, demand
  indicators, and engagement velocity — and to generate written summary reports.
- Reports contain aggregated insights and, at most, a small number of cited
  public permalinks. We do not republish or redistribute platform content in bulk.

## Data retention & deletion
- We store only what is needed to produce a report: content identifiers, public
  metric snapshots over time, and short excerpts.
- **Raw third-party post content is deleted within 48 hours.** Only derived,
  aggregate insights are retained beyond that window.
- If content is removed or made private on the source platform, our corresponding
  copy is purged on the next sampling cycle.
- We honor data-deletion requests; contact the email above.

## Sharing
- We do **not** sell, license, or redistribute platform data.
- Data is not used to train foundation/AI models.
- We use third-party processors (e.g., Supabase for storage, Apify for public
  data collection, Anthropic for summarization) solely to operate the tool.

## Security
- Access is restricted to the operator. Credentials and data are stored in a
  private database and are not exposed publicly.

## Changes
- We may update this policy; the "last updated" date reflects the latest version.
